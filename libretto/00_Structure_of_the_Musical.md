# Structure of the Musical  
### *Muse and Poet: Mosaic of Worlds*

This file defines the dramatic and musical architecture of the project.

## Current Confirmed Sections:

1. **Prologue** – introduction of the Poet’s inner world and the metaphysics of inspiration.  
2. **Duet “Two Voices”** – dialogue revealing the emotional core of the Poet and his beloved.  
3. **Interlude (Night)** – a musical transformation preceding the final revelation.  
4. **Finale (Short Version)** – the canonical stage finale sung by the Muse.  
5. **Finale (Long Version)** – the symphonic or concert finale.

More sections will be added as the libretto evolves.
